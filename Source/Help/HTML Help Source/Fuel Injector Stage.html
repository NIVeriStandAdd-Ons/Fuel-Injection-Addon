Stage Analysis Results

This section contains the analysis results for a given stage within an injection shot. Note: not all channels will be present based on your selected settings.

Injection Event Analysis Channels

Pull In Time: The time in microseconds (us) from the start of the Pull In section to the start of the Hold In section.

Pull In Max Current: The maximum current in amps (A) found during the Pull In section.

Hold In Time: The time in microseconds (us) from the start of the Hold In section to the start of the Off section.

Hold In Max Current: The maximum current in amps (A) found during the Hold In section.

Hold In Min Current: The minimum current in amps (A) found during the Hold In section.

Hold In Avg Current: The average current in amps (A) found during the Hold In section.

Hold In Avg Max Current: The peaks of the current signal are found during the Hold In section. The maximum value of each peak is then taken and averaged in amps (A).

Hold In Avg Min Current: The valleys of the current signal are found during the Hold In section. The minimum value of each valley is then taken and averaged in amps (A).

Hold In Max Voltage: The maximum voltage in volts (V) found during the Hold In section.

Hold In Min Voltage: The minimum voltage in volts (V) found during the Hold In section.

Hold In Avg Voltage: The average voltage in volts (V) found during the Hold In section.

Hold In Avg Max Voltage: The peaks of the voltage signal are found during the Hold In section. The maximum value of each peak is then taken and averaged in voltage (V).

Hold In Avg Min Voltage: The valleys of the voltage signal are found during the Hold In section. The minimum value of each valley is then taken and averaged in voltage (V).

Off Time: The time in microseconds (us) from the start of the Off section to the end of the Off section.

Off Stage Max Current: The maximum current in amps (A) found during the Off section.

Off Stage Min Current: The minimum current in amps (A) found during the Off section.

Off Stage Avg Current: The average current in amps (A) found during the Off section.

Total Voltage Pulses: The number of voltage pulses found during the entire stage (includes all three sections).
